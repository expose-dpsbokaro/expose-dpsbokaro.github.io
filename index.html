The DPS Bokaro

The 789, a vigilante group dedicated to investigative journalism, is committed to shining a light on the internal discipline issues and scandals occurring at DPS Bokaro, one of the most 'reputed' schools in Bokaro. The group firmly believes that exposing the harsh realities of this institution is crucial, as it could help parents and students make informed decisions and contribute to the overall improvement of the education system. The 789 group has taken it upon themselves to uncover the truth about the school's practices, which we believe is misleading parents and students through false advertising.

By bringing these issues to the forefront, the group aims to empower parents and students to make more informed choices about their educational options, ultimately leading to a better educational experience for all.
